Treat yourself to a freshly baked Uppaal from [Downloads][14]!

See the [new features][15] from TIGA and Stratego.

Uppaal is an integrated tool environment for modeling, validation and verification of real-time
systems modeled as networks of timed automata, extended with data types (bounded integers, arrays,
etc.).

The tool is developed in collaboration between the [Department of Information Technology][16] at
Uppsala University, Sweden and the [Department of Computer Science][17] at Aalborg University in
Denmark.
