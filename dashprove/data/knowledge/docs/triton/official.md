# Welcome to Triton’s documentation![¶][2]

[Triton][3] is a language and compiler for parallel programming. It aims to provide a Python-based
programming environment for productively writing custom DNN compute kernels capable of running at
maximal throughput on modern GPU hardware.

## Getting Started[¶][4]

* Follow the [installation instructions][5] for your platform of choice.
* Take a look at the [tutorials][6] to learn how to write your first Triton program.
