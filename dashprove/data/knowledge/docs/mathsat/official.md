## Introduction                                                                     │Contents       
                                                                                    │[Home][8]      
Welcome to the home page of MathSAT 5, an efficient [Satisfiability modulo theories │[People][9]    
(SMT)][4] solver. MathSAT 5 is the successor of [MathSAT 4][5], supporting a wide   │[Documentation]
range of theories (including e.g. equality and uninterpreted functions, linear      │[10]           
arithmetic, bit-vectors, and arrays) and functionalities (including e.g. computation│[Download][11] 
of Craig interpolants, extraction of unsatisfiable cores, generation of models and  │[Publications][
proofs, and the ability of working incrementally).                                  │12]            
                                                                                    │[Links][13]    
MathSAT 5 is a joint project of [Fondazione Bruno Kessler][6] and [DISI-University  │               
of Trento][7].                                                                      │
