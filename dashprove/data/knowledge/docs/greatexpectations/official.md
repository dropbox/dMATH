### Built on open source, powered by community

The open-source standard for data quality testing

GX Core is a flexible, Python-based framework that fits right into your data workflows. Write tests
that reflect your business logic, validate data where it lives, and get structured results you can
plug into CI/CD, alerting, or dashboards.

Catch issues during development, validate data before it moves downstream, and monitor quality in
production—without rewriting your workflows.
