## [21st KeY Symposium 2025][1]

The KeY Symposium brings together researchers interested in KeY and related aspects. We will
exchange recent achievements, current ideas, discuss the next steps and milestones of the area, as
well as future directions in general. Also the latest developments in the KeY tool are presented and
discussed.

This year the KeY Symposium takes place from the 5th August to 8th August in Manigod, France.
